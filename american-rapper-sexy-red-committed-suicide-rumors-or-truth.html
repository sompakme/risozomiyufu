American Rapper Sexy Red Committed Suicide: Rumors or Truth?

Publish date: 2024-09-09

Did Sexy Red commit suicide? The truth was exposed!

Rumors of American rapper Sexy Red committing suicide were circulating the Internet. Is it true?

Sexy Red did not commit suicide. The news of Sexy Red committing suicide was untrue.

The confusion stemmed from a shooting incident near a music video set for rapper Sexy Red in Florida. The Broward County Sheriff’s Office responded to a call about a shooting shortly before 8 p.m. at the 3100 block of W. Oakland Park Boulevard, near Oakland Park Flea Marked.

The authorities found two individuals with apparent gunshot wounds. One person died at the scene, while the other was transferred to the hospital. The identities of the victims were not released.

According to the Sheriff’s Office, the gunfire happened “near a video shoot being filmed in the area.” The incident remains under investigation.
Sexy Red offered her condolences for the deceased victim and prayers for the wounded in a post on her social media. She clarified to her fans that the shooting happened at a gas station across the street from her video set, and she was safe. The Florida Highway Patrol and Broward County Sheriff confirmed that the gunshot incident did not happen on her set.

Who is Sexy Red?

Sexy Red is an American rapper. She was born on April 15, 1998. Her real name is Janae Nierah Wherry. She grew up in St. Louis County, Missouri. She has a son. She adopted Sexy Red as her stage name because she admired her appearance, and Red is her favorite color.

She rose to fame in recent years with her several hit songs. In January 2023, she released her song “Pound Town” with Tay Keith, which went viral online. In May, she released a “Pound Town 2” remix with Nicki Minaj. It was her first song to make the Billboard Hot 100. In June, she released “Hood Hottest Princes,” which received praise. In August 2023, she appeared as a guest at shows with Drake and performed as an opening act on his “It’s All a Blur Tour.”

Billboard announced her as “one of the biggest breakout artist of summer 2023”. She was nominated for “Best Breakthrough Hip-Hop Artist” at the 2023 BET Hip Hop Awards. Her public persona exudes confidence, and her authentic lyrics resonate with her audience. She is a rising artist with immense talent. She has proved herself in a competitive music industry in a very short time. She has mesmerized everyone with her talents.
