Ariana Madix News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Name:Ariana Madix

Hometown:Melbourne, Florida

Relationship:Single

Birthday:June 24, 1985

About

Ariana Madix was born and raised in Florida. She graduated from Flagler College in 2007 and appeared in her first short film, Swing Vote: What Side Are you On?, the following year.

While working at SUR and Villa Blanca in Los Angeles, Madix made a brief cameo on season 1 of Vanderpump Rules in 2013. Following appearance on season 2, she was upgraded to full-time cast member during season 3 in 2014.

The actress has since appeared on several episodes of CollegeHumor Originals and had a few guest starring roles on Anger Management, Lonely and Horny, Waking Up With Strangers and Paradise City.

The TV personality confirmed her relationship with Tom Sandoval in early 2014. Five years later, the duo cowrote Fancy AF Cocktails: Drink Recipes from a Couple of Professional Drinkers. In March 2023, Us Weekly confirmed that Madix and Sandoval split after the TomTom cofounder had an affair with their costar Raquel Leviss.
