Vince McMahon Nearly Replaced Stephanie McMahon At SummerSlam Autograph Signing

Publish date: 2024-07-14

Mike Johnson of PW Insider reports that Vince McMahon was prepared to replace Stephanie McMahon at a SummerSlam related autograph session at the Barclays Center.

Stephanie McMahon had attended Jim “The Anvil” Neidhart’s funeral; but, was questionable to return for her scheduled autograph signing with Triple H due to inclement weather.

Fans in line were informed about the dilemma and also told that if Stephanie was unable to make the signing she would be replaced by WWE CEO Vince McMahon.

Ultimately, Stephanie McMahon was able to attend the signing alongside her husband Triple H and the event went on as originally planned.
