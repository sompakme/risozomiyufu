'Veep': The Cast Ranked From Richest To Poorest

Publish date: 2024-09-13

Veep is an ironic TV show about politics that ran on HBO from 2012 to 2019. The series gives a funny behind-the-scenes look at American politics through the eyes of its main character, Selina Meyer, who is played by Julia Louis-Dreyfus. Selina Meyer, who starts out as Vice President of the United States and ends up as President. However, her journey is anything but easy as she and her team try to find their way through Washington, D.C. The show looks at the power struggles, egos, and, with this crew, hilarious things that happen in politics.

Throughout the series, Meyer faces a never-ending stream of problems, from international crises to political scandals to personal problems. The entire time, her loyal staff, ambitious coworkers, and, of course, the media are there to see how she handles it.

10 Julia Louis-Dreyfus As Selina Meyer In Veep ($250 Million Net Worth)

Julia Louis-Dreyfus's role as Selina Meyer was a turning point in her career because it was a lead role that truly showed how hysterical she is. Selina Meyer is the show's main character, and her story undergoes significant changes throughout the series. She starts as the Vice President of the United States and later becomes the President. Louis-Dreyfus gave the character nuances, depth, and realism, which made Selina both likable and hilariously flawed.

One of the best parts of the show was how well-timed her jokes were and how sharp and biting her one-liners were. Selina's profane rants and memorable insults became signature traits of the character, and the audience ate them up. Louis-Dreyfus made Selina more than just a political stereotype, and honestly, it was a refreshing POV.

Also, Julia Louis-Dreyfus' portrayal of Selina Meyer set a record for the number of Emmy Awards won by a performer in the same role. Not only that, but even better, her role in Veep allowed her to open up about her cancer diagnosis with the cast and crew, who in turn supported her immensely through her battle. With all that she is and all that she has done, it is no wonder that Julia Louis-Dreyfus has a massive net worth of $250 million.

9 Tony Hale As Gary Walsh In Veep ($9 Million Net Worth)

The way Tony Hale plays Gary Walsh on Veep can only be described as a brilliant piece of comedy. Gary is the personal assistant to Selina Meyer while she is VP and also when she becomes president. He is known for his loyalty, his odd behavior, and his deep love for Selina.

Gary is a fan favorite because Tony Hale gives him a unique mix of weirdness and endearing charm that makes him stand out. Gary is always by Selina's side, pays close attention to every detail, and cares deeply about her. This makes for many funny and heartwarming moments throughout the series.

8 Anna Chlumsky As Amy Brookheimer In Veep ($5 Million Net Worth)

Anna Chlumsky's character Amy Brookheimer works as Selina Meyer's Chief of Staff. She is smart, works hard, and is fiercely loyal to Selina, often going to great lengths to help her succeed. Amy Brookheimer is known for her deadpan humor. She often had some cutting zingers and sarcastic comments that would make you laugh in the midst of political chaos. Her ability to make people laugh through subtlety and understatement made her a fan favorite.

7 Timothy Simons As Jonah Ryan In Veep ($5 Million Net Worth)

Timothy Simons' character in Veep, Jonah Ryan, starts out as a liaison for the White House and later becomes a member of Congress. He is shown to be rude, ignorant, and always unfortunate, which makes him an easy target for jokes. In the show, Jonah's character is a source of humor, and his interactions with other characters are often hysterical in a weird way.

6 Sufe Bradshaw As Sue Wilson In Veep ($5 Million Net Worth)

Sufe Bradshaw's character in Veep, Sue Wilson, is Selina Meyer's secretary. She is very proficient at her job and literally never gets upset. Sue is straight-forward and keeps the office running well. With her dry sense of humor, she fits in well with the rest of the cast. Despite her stoic demeanor, Sue is also fiercely loyal to Selina and will go to great lengths to protect her and the team. Sufe Bradshaw has held several movie roles, all of which contributed to her $5 million net worth.

5 Gary Cole As Kent Davison In Veep ($5 Million Net Worth)

Gary Cole plays Kent Davison, the Director of Communications and Senior Strategist for Selina Meyer on Veep. He is a very smart and data-driven political operative who often speaks quickly and in a lot of big words. Kent makes the show more interesting, and like the rest of the team, he is very dedicated to his boss.

Gary Cole is also known for his roles in Talladega Nights: The Ballad of Ricky Bobby and in NCIS as Alden Parker. Cole is a seasoned actor, both on-screen and in voiceovers, with over 180 credits to his name, all contributing to his $5 million net worth.

4 Kevin Dunn As Ben Cafferty In Veep ($4 Million Net Worth)

Kevin Dunn's Ben Cafferty is the White House Chief of Staff and a seasoned political operative on Veep. He is known for his gruff demeanor and cynical outlook on politics. Ben's character provides a counterbalance to the chaos of Selina Meyer's team, offering pragmatic and often darkly humorous insights.

Aside from the 54 episodes Kevin Dunn starred on Veep for, the actor's net worth also grew from his appearances on other television show's including Melissa McCarthy's God's Favorite Idiot.

3 Matt Walsh As Mike McLintock In Veep ($4 Million Net Worth)

Matt Walsh's Mike McLintock is in charge of Selina Meyer's communications on Veep. He wants to do a good job, but he often doesn't. Like when Mike talks to the media, he makes a lot of mistakes that come back on Selina and/or the team. Even though Mike slips up all the time, fans love him because of how kind he is and how loyal he is to Selina. He has funny moments and the ability to make even the worst situations into laugh-out-loud moments. Matt Walsh's net worth of $4 million is largely due to his very successful acting career.

2 Reid Scott As Dan Egan In Veep ($3 Million Net Worth)

Reid Scott's character, Dan Egan, starts out as a White House liaison for Vice President Selina Meyer on Veep. Dan is a self-serving political strategist who is known for his ambition and manipulation. He is a central character in the show and offers a different dynamic to the team surrounding Selina with his cunning and unapologetic nature.

Scott appeared in all 65 episodes of Veep, in addition to several other major acting roles in other television series, including the 2006-2010 series, My Boys alongside Jordana Spiro.

1 Sarah Sutherland As Catherine Meyer In Veep ($2 Million Net Worth)

Sarah Sutherland plays Catherine Meyer, the daughter of Selina on Veep. She is often portrayed as being cold and less than interested in her mother's work in politics. Her mom and the team often try to get her involved in different campaign events, but Catherine could honestly care less. Selina comes off like she could also care less, but in reality, she is very worried about her daughter, and it stresses her out.

Their tense relationship makes Selina's already complicated political life even more complicated. Despite their relationship on the show, behind the scenes Sarah and Julia have amazing chemistry and have a wonderful time laughing and working together. After Veep, Sarah Sutherland went on to star in a couple of smaller movie roles.
