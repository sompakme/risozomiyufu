Alex Glaze 11Alive

After graduation, Alex started working as a production assistant at WBFF-TV in Baltimore, Maryland, before accepting his first on-air job at WNWO-TV. He joined the 11Alive team in March 2017. He comes to Atlanta following two years as the weekend sports anchor for WNWO-TV in Toledo, Ohio.

During his days in Toledo, Glaze covered the NBA Finals, the World Series, Ohio State, and Michigan athletics in addition to several other local university and prep sports. Besides, he won the Ohio Associated Press award for Best Sports Feature in 2015. Furthermore, he was nominated for the Toledo Press Club’s Excellence in Journalism Touchstone award.
