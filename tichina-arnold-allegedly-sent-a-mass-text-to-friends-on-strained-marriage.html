Tichina Arnold Allegedly Sent A Mass Text To Friends On Strained Marriage

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Tichina Arnold, popularly known for her role as Pam, the always-upset, best friend to Tisha Campbell’s character (Gina) on Martin, is dealing with real life issues that transpires outside of the silver screen.  TMZ reports that the actress sent out a mass text to friends and family, announcing that her marriage is over due to her husband’s infidelity.

“I can’t for the life of me understand why Rico would video himself [f–king] ‘raw dog’ ONE of his MANY extraneous whores that ‘trick’ on him for his money,” the mass text allegedly stated. Arnold married her husband, Rico Hines, who formerly coached St. John’s University’s basketball team, in 2012.

But briefly following their happy-ever-after, Arnold alleged him of having unprotected sex with several women. And this is where she decided to draw the line. “This is where I part ways and all bets are off,” she said.

Arnold reportedly continued, listing potentially 20 women who’ve engaged in relations with Hines, calling them, “extraneous Kim Kardashian wanna be’s.” According to TMZ, she also expressed concern about possibly contracting a sexually transmitted disease.

When TMZ reached out to the other party, Hines’ team stated, “This is a private and deeply personal matter between my wife and me, and I do not want to comment any further at this time.”
