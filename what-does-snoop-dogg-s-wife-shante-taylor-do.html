Who Is Shante Broadus & What Does Her Married Life Look Like?

Shante Broadus was born in California in 1971. She went to Long Beach Polytechnic High School where she met Calvin Cordozar Broadus Jr. – the guy that soon became Snoop Dogg. They fell in love and were then married in 1997 and have a family together. Despite nearly divorcing, the pair has renewed their vows and persevered through the difficult times.

Snoop filed for divorce from Shante in 2004 for “irreconcilable differences,” but he didn’t go through with it in the end. The rapper admitted that he went back home and “fell back in love” with his wife and their children. The couple also credited their marriage lasting because of their daughter Cori’s diagnosis with Lupus.

Of course, that came after their 'break' when Snoop Dogg left his wife for a time, and was aiming to follow a different career path from the one he's on today.

What Does Shante Taylor Broadus Do?

Shante Broadus has an impressive resume that includes making wonderful things happen for bright people. She officially became Snoop Dogg's manager in 2021, which is a significant task considering the musician's involvement in TV, apparel, cannabis, video games, mobile apps, and a lot more.

Still, being her husband’s manager is clearly a role she’s been preparing for since the ’90s. “Shante has been guiding my career behind the scenes from day one. She has always been my final gut check with all my decisions, and I wouldn’t be where I am today without her. I am grateful to have her as my official manager, helping to build the Snoop Dogg empire together,” Snoop said in a press release cited by Forbes.

Shante also couldn’t be happier with the role, she told in an interview: “He knows that he can always trust me. So he wanted the world to know he had his wife by his side, helping him. He wanted a strong female to lead. And of course, that’s me. I’m the boss lady.”

Aside from being Snoop’s manager, Shante is also the founder of Boss Lady Entertainment. As the name implies, the company is a powerhouse that, among other endeavors, helms operation for a Los Angeles recording and events complex called The Compound that draws such top-notch talent like Cardi B and Kendrick Lamar.

Shante was also executive producer on the 2012 Snoop documentary titled, Reincarnated, was part of the E! reality show, Snoop Dogg’s Fatherhood. Moreover, she has a number of brands under her belt and has a new venture called The Broadus Collection – a collection of scented candles. To add to the list of her achievements, she’s also the CEO of Boss Lady Spirits.

Shante Broadus, who has a net worth of $5 million, is proof that it’s not where you came from that matters — it’s where you’re going, and what you do with what you’ve been given. She’s been through a lot — personally and professionally — yet persevered to make a name for herself, by herself.

Most of all, she’s more than just Snoop Dogg’s wife — she’s a mother, grandmother, and consummate professional, and that alone makes her story both aspirational and inspirational. And that makes people love her!
