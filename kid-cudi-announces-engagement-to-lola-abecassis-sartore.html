Kid Cudi Announces Engagement To Lola Abecassis Sartore

Publish date: 2024-07-14

The Man on the Moon is jumping the broom: Kid Cudi has announced that he’s getting married to his girlfriend, Lola Abecassis Sartore.

On Thursday morning (April 18), the music artist and actor took to social media to share the news of the latest development in his love life. Sharing a recent photo of himself and Sartore visibly in good spirits, Cudi professed his affections for his fiancée, crediting her with increasing his joy and being a perfect long-term mate.

“This amazing woman makes me so happy, she is everything to me and im so excited to share this news w u all,” the Ohio native told his fans. “Life is wild, and right when I felt my future was uncertain, it became crystal clear with Lola. Happy Cud in full effect.”

In a separate post on her own Instagram account, Sartore spoke glowingly of Cudi, declaring that she “never thought I could love someone this much” while deeming herself a “proud fiancée.” In a comment under Sartore’s post, the hitmaker vowed that he would be hers “forever.”

While it’s unclear how long the pair have been an item, Sartore, a menswear designer for Louis Vuitton, looks to be the missing piece Cudi has been searching for in recent years, as he previously expressed his desire to be in a committed relationship.

“I want a girlfriend,” the 40-year-old told Esquire in 2022. “I need to be with somebody. Most people would think I enjoy being single, because I’m single a lot. But I’m a relationship man… My goal is to find someone. Soon, hopefully. And get married and have more kids.”

Earlier this week, Cudi and Sartore made their first publicized appearance together while attending the afterparty for the global premiere of the Paramount+ series Knuckles, which Cudi stars in, on Tuesday (April 16) in London, England.

In other Cudi news, this weekend, the hitmaker will be performing at Coachella as a last-minute addition to the festival’s vast lineup. On Sunday (April 21), he’ll be taking the stage at the Sahara tent, adding to an explosive lineup of superstars such as Doja Cat, Lil Uzi Vert, Lil Yachty, Tyler, the Creator, No Doubt, Lana Del Rey, and more.
