VH1's Hip Hop Honors Adds Lil Kim To List Of Tributes

Publish date: 2024-09-10

This year’s triumphant return of VH1’s Hip Hop Honors celebration has added Lil Kim to their growing number of tributes to the queens of the genre.

VH1 Hip Hop Honors: All Hail The Queens will highlight the entertainer’s illustrious career in music and fashion, Billboard reports. The Grammy-winner has created accolades in the genre for female rappers with her three No. 1 albums, Hard Core, Notorious K.I.M. and La Bella Mafia. Other honorees include Missy Elliott, Queen Latifah and Salt-N-Pepa featuring Spinderella.

The rapper was happy to celebrate the honor on her Instagram page on Thursday (June 16.)
