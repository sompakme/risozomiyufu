Relive Undertaker vs. Jeff Hardy

Jeff Hardy became a legendary figure in the world of professional wrestling thanks to his extreme moves, so he has no problem performing in Extreme Rules Matches. In 2008, Hardy stepped into the ring against the iconic Undertaker, to take on the legend in an Extreme Rules Match that became an instant classic for the SmackDown brand. Ahead of tomorrow’s pay-per-view, you can check out the past match between Hardy and the Undertaker in its entirety, courtesy of WWE’s YouTube account.
