The Richest Cast Members Of 'Fear Factor Season 6', Ranked

Adopted from the original Dutch series Now or Neverland, Fear Factor is a reality stunt game show which aired on NBC from 2001-2006 and involved a group of people/celebrities performing spine-chilling stunts for prize money. The first five seasons followed a format in which three men and three women competed against each other in 3 different stunts to win the $50,000 prize money. But the show's format took a sharp turn in the sixth season, which featured four teams with two people each having pre-existing relationships with each other.

Joe Rogan hosted Fear Factor, and the sixth season featured a special Celebrity Fear Factor episode that featured several famous celebrities from the industry. Below is a list ranking all the stars featured in Fear Factor Season 6 according to their net worth.

Trishelle Cannatella - $500,000

Trishelle Cannatella is a PlayBoy model, actress, and reality TV show contestant known for her illustrious modeling career and multiple appearances in reality TV shows. Trishelle started modeling at just 15 and went on to pose nude for Playboy magazine. She appeared as a reality star in Season 6 Episode 22 of Fear Factor. Apart from this, she has also appeared in reality shows like The Challenge, Surreal Life, Punk'd, etc., and has appeared in a few movies. Trishelle currently has a modest net worth of $500,000.

Traci Bingham - $2 Million

An actress, model, and TV personality, Traci Bingham began her career during the mid-90s appearing in small movie and TV roles. She rose to fame after appearing in the hit TV show Baywatch as Jordan Tate. Since then, she has appeared in multiple reality TV shows like Celebrity Big Brother, The Surreal Life: Fame Games, To Tell the Truth, etc. Apart from TV, she has also modeled for Playboy magazine. Traci has garnered a net worth of $2 Million to date.

Brande Roderick - $3 Million

The gorgeous Brande Roderick is a model and actress best known for playing the role of lifeguard Leigh Dyer in the 11th season of the cult TV show Baywatch. Apart from slaying the audience with her performance in Baywatch, her modeling career boomed in 2000 when she appeared in Playboy magazine's 'playmate of the month,' followed by becoming playmate of the year in 2001. Brande Roderick appeared in the 22nd episode of Fear Factor 6 and currently has an impressive net worth of $3 Million.

Gordon Liddy - $5 Million

Probably the most controversial figure in this list, G. Gordon Liddy was many things, including a lawyer, FBI agent, talk show host, actor, and convicted felon in the Watergate scandal of the White House, which led to the downfall of ex-POTUS Richard Nixon. After getting jailed for several years, he restarted his career as a radio talk show host from 1992 to 2012. He earned a lot during his stint on the radio and accumulated a net worth of $5 Million during his death in 2021 as per Celebrity Net Worth.

Tempestt Bledsoe - $6 Million

Tempestt Bledsoe is an actress best known for her childhood role as Vanessa Huxtable on the popular NBC sitcom The Cosby Show, appearing in 157 of the show’s 201 episodes. After her role in The Cosby Show, Bledsoe starred in several TV shows and movies, including Clean House, Afterschool Special, The Parkers, The Expendables, and Family Reunion. Bledsoe also appeared as a self-contestant in the celebrity special episode of Fear Factor Season 6. Over a long-spanning TV career, Bledsoe has piled a handsome net worth of $6 Million.

Arianne Zucker - $7 Million

One of the most famous celebs on this list, Arianne Zucker is a three-time Emmy-nominated actress and model known for her lead role as Nicole Walker in the 1998 soap opera Days of Our Lives. As per IMDB, she has received three Daytime Emmy nominations and one Soap Opera Digest Award win for her fantastic portrayal of Nicole Walker. She starred in TV shows like CSI: Miami and DeVanity, along with her appearance in the 6th Edition of Fear Factor. Arianne has had a long career in TV, which has helped her accumulate an impressive $7 Million net worth.

The Miz - $14 Million

Michael Gregory Mizanin, known mainly by his WWE stage name, The Miz, is a professional wrestler and actor. Before starting his wrestling career in WWE, Miz starred in reality TV shows like The Real World: Back to New York and Real World/Road Rules Challenge. He debuted in the WWE in 2006 and became a two-time WWE Champion and eight-time Intercontinental Champion. Apart from a successful WWE career, The Miz has also appeared in movies and TV shows like The Marine 4: Moving Target, Christmas Bounty, Cannonball, etc. He also appeared in Fear Factor 6 in 3 episodes and was the winner. The Miz, over a long and successful career in WWE and Hollywood, has made a massive fortune of $14 Million, as reported by First Sportz.

Joe Rogan - $120 Million

The heart, soul, and host of all the seasons of Fear Factor, Joe Rogan, is the most famous and richest celebrity on the list. He has a multi-talented portfolio as a UFC color commentator, podcaster, comedian, actor, and former television presenter. He hosted all six seasons of Fear Factor from 2001-2006, appearing in every episode. After Fear Factor, Joe started focusing on stand-up comedy and currently hosts one of the most famous podcasts in the world, The Joe Rogan Experience. The distribution rights of Joe’s podcast were acquired by Spotify in 2020 for a whopping $200 Million, and in the same year, he cashed out a staggering $14.4 Million for a swanky mansion in Austin, Texas. He boasts a humongous net worth of $120 Million, as reported by Wealthy Gorilla.

Season 6 of Fear Factor featured some of the most famous celebrities during its release in 2006. Some stars went on to have hugely successful careers and garnered multi-million dollar net worths. The host of Fear Factor, Joe Rogan, is the richest on the list with a staggering net worth of $120 Million, thus beating everyone else by a considerable margin.
