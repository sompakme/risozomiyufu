    Alex Gaskarth All Time

    Alex began playing music with bandmate Jack Barakat in eighth grade. In ninth grade, Zack Merrick and Rian Dawson joined them, forming All Time Low in 2003. In 2004 they signed to Emerald Moon Records while still in high school and released their debut EP, “The Three Words to Remember in Dealing with the End”. They released their debut studio album The Party Scene in 2005.

    The band signed with Hopeless Records in 2006. They released a Platinum single “Dear Maria, Count Me In”in 2007 and it was certified 4x Platinum by Billboard in 2008. In 2007 they released their second studio album So Wrong, It’s Right. All Time Low released their third studio album Nothing Personal in 2009 which debuted at number 4 on Billboard 200.

    The band released two CD/DVD packages, MTV Unplugged and the documentary/live concert Straight to DVD in 2010. The band signed with Interscope Records in 2011 and released their fourth studio album Dirty Work. The band released a new song entitled “Dirty Laundry” in 2017 and also reveals that they had left Hopeless Records and signed with Fueled by Ramen one year prior.

    The band released its seventh studio album Last Young Renegade on 2 June 2017. The band released two new singles, Everything Is Fine and Birthday in the summer of 2018. Alex has written songs for/with other bands such as 5 Seconds of Summer, McBusted,  and Simple Plan. He has also been a host of the podcast Full Frontal with bandmate Jack Barakat since 2013.

    Alex Gaskarth Simple Creatures

    On 24 January 2019, Alex announced he was forming a new group with Mark Hoppus of Blink-182 called Simple Creatures. A debut song named “Drug” was released with news of an upcoming EP slated for March. A single named “Strange Love” was also released.
