Shaher Abdulhak Wikipedia and Job Explored

Shaher Abdulhak was born in 1938 in Taiz, Yemen. As he was media-shy, many details of his early life background and educational qualifications were not shared.

Abdulhak never granted interviews, and no photo of him was ever printed in the local press.

It was not until December 2009 that his picture was shown for the first time in public media when Norwegian newspaper VG printed a private photograph of his.

Abdulhak passed away on October 2, 2020, due to cancer in Germany. Per the reports, he was buried in Cairo, Egypt, on October 6.
