Alex Pietrangelo is a Canadian professional ice hockey player which has an estimated net worth of $4.6 million.

Alex Pietrangelo began his career when he played for minor hockey in Toronto. Then, he joined the OMHA's Richmond Hill Stars and spent three years with the GTHL's Vaughn Kings.

Before he landed a spot in NHL, Pietrangelo gone to the 2006 OHL Priority Selection and chosen by the Mississauga IceDogs. Two years later, he was drafted by the St. Louis Blues during the 2008 NHL Entry Draft. He played his first full season with the Blues during the 2011-2012 season.
