Paul Washer Biography

Paul Washer is an acclaimed American Protestant Evangelist, Missionary and Preacher who was born and brought-up in the United States. He does his missions with the Calvinist theology associated with the Southern Baptist Convention. He is the Director and Administrator of HeartCry Missionary Society.

Paul converted to Christianity while studying to become an oil and gas lawyer at the University of Texas at Austin. Paul moved to Peru and became a missionary there for 10 years. While in Peru in 1988, he founded the HeartCry Missionary Society to support indigenous missionaries witnessing to people of their own culture.

Paul Washer Church

According to reports, Washer converted to Christianity at the University of Texas at Austin, while studying to be an oil and gas lawyer. Washer moved to Peru where he became a missionary for a decade. While in Peru, Washer founded the HeartCry Missionary Society in 1988, to support indigenous missionaries witnessing to people of their own culture.

Washer returned to the United States of America and he resides in Radford, Virginia since 2010, where he continues as the missions director of HeartCry, a ministry caring for the needy in many countries. Washer is sometimes identified as part of the New Calvinism movement, although he has expressed concerns with this movement.
