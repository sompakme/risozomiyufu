Who Is Aron Piper?

Aron Piper became an international heartthrob following his debut on the hit Netflix series, Elite. The show first premiered back in 2018 and has launched the careers of various cast members, including Ester Exposito, Itzan Escamilla, and Miguel Bernardeau, to name a few.

As the show enters its fourth season, fans have been growing more and more in love with Aron Piper's character, Ander Muñoz. While Ander's life is full of mischief, and mystery, he remains a fan favorite, mainly when it comes to his screen stealing romance with Omar, played by Omar Ayuso.

While Elite is certainly the role that catapulted the actor to great heights, he's been acting for quite some time! After moving from Germany to Spain at 5 years old, Aron took a liking to the arts from a very young age.

He began acting around 8 years before fully committing to studying acting and directing throughout his time in high school and university.

Prior to his time working on the Netflix show, Aron appearing in a number of films, including The Gunman (2004), 15 Years And One Day (2013), and in the hit Spanish series, Centro Médico (2016), to name a few!

Since his start, Aron Piper has amassed a net worth of an estimated $1 million, thanks to his successful role in the classic drama series.

In addition to his work when it comes to acting, Aron is also making strides in the world of music! Prior to the pandemic, Piper shared a video of himself onto his Instagram in the recording studio.

Although he hasn't given out too many details regarding what he might be working on, it's clear his interest in music will certainly lead to something great, and fans cannot wait!

When it comes to Aron's personal life, there isn't too much that's known! The actor keeps much on the down-low, especially when it comes to his dating life.

While that aspect of his life remains a mystery, the actor has proven himself to be a fashion and travel enthusiast, further adding to Piper's long list of attributes that fans adore!
