'Impractical Jokers' Partners And Current Net Worth, Revealed

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Impractical Jokers is a reality television series on TruTV that follows four best friends: Sal Vulcano, Joe Gatto, Brian Quinn, and James Murray, who go by Sal, Joe, Q, and Murr… respectively. This rowdy group has one mission, and that is to embarrass the others as much as possible. With different tasks and missions that push them into discomfort, whoever fails the most during the episode is awarded a personalized punishment.

The show debuted in December 2011 and is now in its tenth season. It is the longest running and highest rated television series aired on the TruTV channel. After being televised for so many years, the show has gained thousands of fans, many of whom are curious about the guys’ personal lives. Here are the current relationship statuses and net worths of the Impractical Jokers.

8 Sal Vulcano Has A $7 Million Net Worth

Sal Vulcano is a stand-up comedian whose claim to fame was with his pals, the Impractical Jokers. He and his friends formed a comedy group called The Tenderloins that toured around the country performing stand-up shows for adoring audiences. Besides that, he has been involved in every Impractical Jokers TV production, raising his net worth to $7 million.

7 Sal Vulcano Might Have A Secret Wife

Because of his kind demeanor, Sal quickly became a fan favorite of the Impractical Jokers fellas. While he is incredibly secretive about his personal life, there are rumors that Vulcano had a secret wedding recently, marrying Francesca Muffaletto. There are little details on this relationship, such as when they started dating or what her social media account is, but if the rumors are true, we send our congrats.

6 Brian Quinn Has A Net Worth Of $7 Million

Brian Quinn has made a name for himself through his usually fearless approach to the tasks his costars give him on the show. He is not only involved in The Tenderloins and the Impractical Jokers productions, but he has also been on a few other projects. Q has starred in two Jay and Silent Bob films, as well as the film Vulgar and a few other animations. These bring his net worth to $7 million.

5 Brian Quinn Is Single And Ready To Mingle

One of the ongoing jokes since Impractical Jokers began filming was that Quinn is “perpetually single.” At one point, his punishment even involved getting a tattoo that included “single” on a list of his attributes. While he may have had fleeting romantic connections throughout the years, he has never been relationally involved with a woman, and continues to be single as of now.

4 James Murray Also Has A $7 Million Net Worth

James Murray is the only joker of the group who has dipped his toe in the art of book writing. He is of course involved in all the Impractical Jokers productions and The Tenderloins stand-up, but he is also an author of four novels. Murray teamed up with his writer friend Darren Wearmouth, and they have been releasing works since 2018, boosting his net worth to $7 million.

3 James Murray Married Melyssa Davies In 2020

In 2019, Murr got down on one knee and proposed to health care worker Melyssa Davies, whom he met about a year prior at a launch party event he was hosting. They fell in love quickly and deeply, setting their wedding plans for 2020. Despite the lockdown, they were able to wed and are now enjoying married life together.

2 Joe Gatto's Net Worth Is $7 Million

Joe Gatto was part of the Impractical Jokers group since the beginning. He traveled with The Tenderloins and performed on all the IJ productions before announcing his exit at the end of 2021. Gatto intends to take this time to focus on his personal life and a solo comedy career, though he holds no ill-will or bad blood with his previous costars. His net worth rests at $7 million.

1 Joe Gatto Is Still Married To His Wife Of 9 Years

In 2013, Joe married his wife Bessy Gatto. Together, they have two children, Milana Francis and Remington Joseph. While the couple announced a separation this past December, they have not yet filed for divorce and have even been spotted together recently with speculations of reconciliation. Joe has not released any details of his current standing with Bessy, but fans are hopeful that the couple will work out their problems.
