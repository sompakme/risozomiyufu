What Is Mini Materials?

Mini Materials is a business that specializes in providing mini construction supplies. They offer a wide range of small-size construction materials, including mini cinder blocks, custom-printed wood pallets & crates, mini bricks, cinder blocks, and red blocks. These mini construction supplies are perfect for building small-scale structures such as tables, houses, walls, and more. With Mini Materials, creativity knows no bounds!

One of the unique aspects of Mini Materials is their commitment to customer satisfaction. In addition to offering high-quality mini construction supplies, the company also provides customers with free miniature blueprints and sand prints of different designs to assist them in their creative endeavors. Whether you’re a DIY enthusiast, a model builder, or someone looking to add a touch of whimsy to your surroundings, Mini Materials has the perfect mini construction supplies for you!

With a focus on precision and craftsmanship, Mini Materials ensures that their products meet the highest standards of quality. Each mini cinder block, wood pallet, and brick is meticulously crafted to ensure durability and authenticity. Mini Materials is passionate about helping their customers bring their miniature construction projects to life.

Who Is The Founder Of Mini Materials?

The founder of Mini Materials is Jared Waters. Jared acquired the business from Mat Hofma and Erik Polumbo in 2022. He brings a diverse range of experiences to the company, including climbing wall supervision, wildland firefighting, and customer service. Prior to owning Mini Materials, Jared worked as a customer service representative at Netflix and in Business Valuation Resources, LLC.

How Was The Shark Tank Pitch Of Mini Materials?

Jared Waters, the founder of Mini Materials, took the stage on Shark Tank to pitch his innovative business idea. He confidently presented Mini Materials to the Sharks, seeking a $100k investment in exchange for 20% equity at a $500k valuation.

“We offer a range of small-size construction materials that have captured the attention of DIY enthusiasts and hobbyists worldwide. Our mini cinder blocks, custom-printed wood pallets & crates, and mini bricks are perfect for building small structures, whether it’s for architectural models or creative projects,”

explained Jared.

The unique concept of Mini Materials caught the attention of the online community, particularly Reddit, where it quickly gained viral popularity. This surge in online buzz translated into a significant increase in sales for the company.

However, despite the success and potential of Mini Materials, none of the Sharks saw it as an investment opportunity. While the Sharks declined to invest in the business, the exposure from appearing on Shark Tank had a positive impact on Mini Materials’ growth and brand awareness.

What Happened To Mini Materials After Shark Tank?

Despite not securing a deal on Shark Tank, Mini Materials continued to thrive and grow under Jared’s leadership. The business experienced a remarkable surge in popularity following the show, leading to a significant increase in sales. Jared’s strategic efforts resulted in a profit of $55,000 from the continued success of Mini Materials.

Mini Materials: A Story of Resilience and Growth

Building on this success, Mini Materials expanded its product range, offering even more mini construction supplies to meet the growing demand. The company also prioritized its marketing efforts, reaching out to a wider audience and attracting new customers.
