Kris Jenner On Kim Kardashian Baby: Shes Beautiful

Publish date: 2024-07-26

Kanye West became a daddy just in time for Father’s Day this past weekend after his girlfriend Kim Kardashian gave birth to the couple’s first baby girl. The mother of the reality clan Kris Jenner stopped by the 40th annual Daytime Emmy Awards in Beverly Hills Sunday night (June 16) to give verbal confirmation that the family is fine and speak on the KimYe bundle of joy. “We’re all fine, we’re all good and extremely happy and thrilled for the new baby,” Kris told HLN’s A.J. Hammer, before adding that the baby was “doing great and she’s beautiful.” While the Kardashian fam has still kept mum about the little one’s name, Khloe said details were coming soon.
