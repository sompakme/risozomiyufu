The Italian Villa From The 'House Of Gucci' Can Now Be Rented On Airbnb

Publish date: 2024-08-10

The House Of Gucci movie and it's all star-cast has been met with rave reviews by fans across the globe. Starring Lady Gaga, Adam Driver, Al Pacino,  and Jeremy Irons, the film has generated award-buzz already, and has left a lasting impression on the cinematic landscape. Those who have watched the opulent lifestyle featured in the film with envy, can now get a first-hand experience at what it's like to live in the mansion that was featured in the film. Villa Balbiano is now up on Airbnb, and eager fans are now flooding the internet in an attempt to secure their reservations.

Fans of The House Of Gucci can now actually step right on to the set where it was filmed, and enjoy a one night's stay within the lavish walls of the Villa Balbiano estate. This is poised to be one of the most real-life movie experiences ever offered to fans, as this Airbnb reservation puts guests right in the center of one of the main areas featured within the popular movie.

Not only does a stay in this massive mansion guarantee an up-close and personal experience for movie fans, it also offers guests a chance to enjoy all the luxurious amenities that this mansion boasts.

Aside from the obvious picturesque appeal of this stunning mansion, and the bragging rights that are sure to be attached to this experience, guests will be able to freely access the first four floors of the huge residence, as well as the perfectly landscaped, vast outdoor grounds as well.

Accessible to visitors will be an enormous private pier and boathouse, perched upon Lake Como, with a perfect view of the water. Lake Como is home to some of Hollywood's biggest celebrities, including George Clooney and his family. As one of the largest lakes in Italy, the view is spectacular, matched only by the beautifully landscaped gardens which surround the villa.  The oversized fountains and a huge swimming pool that were the center of filming during the party scene for Alberto's birthday in the movie, are also available for guests to enjoy.

The Villa is one of the largest private residences located on Lake Como, and features six vast suites and jaw dropping decorative elements. The master suite boasts opulent red and gold furnishings and huge overhanging chandeliers, which ooze luxury at every turn. The bathroom within the master suite comes with marble floors and a number of upgraded features.

Those interested in booking this prestigious venue should act fast. Reservations will be made available for one-night stays as of Monday, December 6th, at the cost of $1,000 euros, and are restricted to two guests at a time.
