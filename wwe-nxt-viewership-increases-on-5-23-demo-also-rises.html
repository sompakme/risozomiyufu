WWE NXT Viewership Increases On 5/23, Demo Also Rises

Publish date: 2024-09-05

 viewership increased on May 23.

According to Wrestlenomics, 578,000 viewers tuned in on Tuesday; this number is up from last week when 564,000 viewers watched the show. The demo rating in the 18-to-49 demographic also increased, as this week’s show scored a 0.16 rating. NXT drew a 0.14 rating in the same demographic last week.

NXT ranked #14 in the demographic among cable originals.

This week’s show kicked off with Lyra Valkyria defeating Cora Jade to advance to the finals of the NXT Women’s Championship tournament. In the main event of the show, Tiffany Stratton defeated Roxanne Perez to advance to the finals of the NXT Women’s Championship tournament this Sunday at NXT Battleground.
