The daughter of Goldie Hawn and step-father Kurt Russell, Kate Hudson was no stranger to movie sets from a young age. She began acting as a teenager, appearing in a Fox drama show before making her film debut shortly after. Her 2001 performance in Almost Famous brought her to prominence and earned her an Oscar nomination. Hudson became a household name for starring in How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days opposite Matthew McConaughey, a movie that has become a classic romantic comedy. Her other notable projects include The Skeleton Key, Nine, Fool’s Gold, Something Borrowed, and Bride Wars. Kate Hudson has been a fixture on the red carpet for many years, and the actress is known for her daring and sophisticated style.

Kate Hudson works with long-time friend and stylist Sophie Lopez, a fashion powerhouse in Los Angeles. The duo has slowly developed Hudson’s style of mixing sheer and lace elements with floor-length ball gowns. Let’s take a look at Kate Hudson’s most expensive red carpet looks.

2015 Golden Globe Awards

 

Kate Hudson arrived in a Jennifer Lopez-inspired Versace dress at the Beverly Hilton Hotel in Los Angeles for the Golden Globe Awards. According to the Huffington Post, the white gown featured a deep V-neckline, a fishtail silhouette, and side panel cut-outs. Her outfit was completed with a pair of diamond earrings and a delicate bracelet around the wrist.

2022 TIFF Glass Onion: A Knives Out Story Premiere

 

Stepping out for the Knives Out sequel screening and premiere at the Toronto International Film Festival, Kate Hudson chose a deep scarlet Carolina Herrera gown. The bold outfit had a body-hugging full-length skirt, a back slit, and a large floral embellishment blooming along the right side of her outfit. She had paired her outfit with matching towering platform heels and layered earrings.

2018 SAG Awards

 

The outfit that caused a lengthy discussion on the internet, Kate Hudson became one of the most talked-about celebrities after her red-carpet appearance at the 2018 SAG Awards. She was dressed in a ruffled pink and black Valentino gown. It featured polka dots on its ruffles, a layered dress, and a full lace collar. Her outfit was completed with a simple black belt around the waistline.

78th Venice Film Festival

 

Kate Hudson turned heads when she arrived on the red carpet for her movie Mona Lisa and the Blood Moon screening at the Venice Film Festival in 2022. She was dressed in a signature scarlet red Valentino gown made with sheer accents, as noted by Marie Claire. The gown had a high collar, cuffed sleeves, and a tulle-stitched full skirt with a small train.

2020 Vanity Fair Oscar Party

 

The Radhika Jones-hosted 2020 Vanity Fair Oscar Party saw Kate Hudson in a timeless yellow and pale blue Vivienne Westwood jacquard gown. It showcased a plunging strapless neckline, a center thigh-high slit, and a mermaid silhouette. She had paired each accessory in a gold shade, including her Jimmy Choo clutch and heels.

2017 SAG Awards

 

Kate Hudson opted for her signature shimmering look at the 2017 SAG Awards in an all-Dior ensemble. Taken from the Pre-Fall collection, it had cut-outs, a deep neckline paired with a sheer cape. She continued the autumn theme of her look with dark makeup and a pinned corsage at the side of her waist. Her outfit was completed with a dark red clutch as an added accessory.

2021 Met Gala

 

Following the 2021 Met Gala In America: A Lexicon of Fashion theme, Kate Hudson chose the classic All-American Girl to look for the exclusive event. As mentioned by Yahoo Style, she was dressed in a Michael Kors light pink bralette, a floor-length feathered coat, and a maxi skirt. She wore Lorraine Schwartz jewels paired with Giuseppe Zanotti heels.

2021 InStyle Awards

 

The 2021 InStyle Awards had a series of A-listers arriving on the red carpet, and Kate Hudson made all heads turn with her glimmering outfit. She wore a sparkling gold Michael Kors gown with a turtle neckline and long sleeves. The actress had completed her look with a camel coat draped over her shoulders and Louboutin heels.

2022 Vanity Fair Oscar Party

 

The actress brought elegance and chic in front of the cameras at the 2022 Vanity Fair Oscar Party. She chose a black strapless Carolina Herrera gown with a full skirt, a train, and a semi-sheer design. The outfit was picked from Herrera’s Fall 2022 collection, and Hudson paired it with earrings from Messika Kashmir and a black clutch.

Mona Lisa and the Blood Moon Premiere

 

After her memorable outfit at the Venice Film Festival, Kate Hudson did not disappoint with her look when she arrived at the LA Premiere of her film. She chose an intricate floor-length Oscar de la Renta gown with sewn flower patterns that enhanced the naked look, as stated by InStyle. The sweetheart neckline delicately accentuated her shoulders, and Hudson opted for a wet-hair look instead of her usual beach waves.

In recent years, Kate Hudson has opted for voluminous outfits with a sheer look that leaves very little to the imagination. As a fashion icon since the early 2000s, Hudson has developed her style and loves to offer input to stylist Sophie Lopez. The actress was recently seen walking the red carpets and promoting her Netflix film Glass Onion: A Knives Out Story.
