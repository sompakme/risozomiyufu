Blac Chyna's Mother Sees Rob Kardashian As Husband Candidate

Publish date: 2024-08-21

It’s looking like Rob Kardashian and Blac Chyna are in it for the long haul – or that’s at least what Blac Chna’s mom, Tokyo, is hoping. In fact, her mom is so into the newfound love connection that she wants the two to get married, according to TMZ.

Tokyo and her husband reportedly paid a visit to Chyna and her grandson King Cairo, and also spent some quality time with Rob. TMZ sources revealed that Tokyo is so glad Rob is in Blac Chyna’s life after having been mistreated by her ex, Tyga, for so long. She reportedly feels that Rob’s positive attitude, good manners, and love for her daughter is the perfect combination for husband material.

But before there are wedding bells for what is seemingly a happy couple, there may be some objections on the Kardashian-Jenner side. After the couple’s dealings dominated headlines in January, reports claimed that the famous family–namely Khloe Kardashian and Tyga’s current girlfriend, Kylie Jenner–were not happy about Rob’s new girl. And Kris Jenner may have joined that band of opposition after reports claimed that she is angered by news of a potential wedding in the making.

The family section may be a little empty at this wedding, but if momma Chyna has anything to say about it, the two will be jumping the broom in the near future. Neither Blac Chyna nor  Rob Kardashian have spoken about an engagement in the making. For right now, it looks like they are just  enjoying working out and making trips to Toy R’ Us with King Cairo.
